All flights on this day x years ago

Be careful with the equality operator ==:

flights %>% 
  filter(month = 6, day = 2)
## Error: `month` (`month = 6`), `day` (`day = 2`) must not be named, do you need `==`?
flights %>% 
  filter(month == 6, day == 2)
